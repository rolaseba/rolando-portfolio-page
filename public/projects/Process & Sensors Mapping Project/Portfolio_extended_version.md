This project focused on developing a **mapping and visualization tool** that enabled the identification and organization of **production processes** and their **physical sensors (PLC tags)** into a clear hierarchical structure.

The tool was **key and necessary to bridge the technical complexity of the plant with the needs of the global digitalization project**, ensuring that detailed local process and sensor information could be standardized and successfully integrated into the corporate software **Aveva PI**, globally used for signal acquisition, capture, and monitoring.

The work allowed both local and global teams to gain a **unified** and **structured** view of processes and their associated signals, facilitating digitalization and integration as part of a **global information unification initiative**.
